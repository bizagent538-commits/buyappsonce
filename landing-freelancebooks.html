Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really a one-time purchase?

Yes! Pay $79 or $149 once and own FreelanceBooks forever. Unlike QuickBooks Self-Employed ($30/mo), FreshBooks ($17/mo), or Wave (free but sells your data), you never pay again.

Does it calculate quarterly estimated taxes?

Yes! FreelanceBooks calculates your estimated quarterly tax payments based on your income and expenses. It uses current self-employment tax rates (15.3%) plus your estimated income tax bracket.

Yes! The Pro version includes professional invoicing with your logo, multiple payment terms, and payment tracking.

Absolutely. FreelanceBooks exports clean reports organized by Schedule C categories. Your tax preparer will love you.
