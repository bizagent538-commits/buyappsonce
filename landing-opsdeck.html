Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between self-hosted and cloud-hosted?

Self-hosted gives you the complete source code and database schema to deploy on your own servers (Vercel + Supabase). You have full control and ownership. Cloud-hosted means we set up and manage everything for you — just log in and use it.

What do I need to run OpsDeck self-hosted?

You'll need a Supabase account (free tier works) and a Vercel account (also free tier). Setup takes about 15 minutes following our documentation. No server management required.

How does SMS notifications work?

SMS for shift notifications uses Twilio. You create your own Twilio account and add your credentials in OpsDeck settings. Twilio charges about $0.0079 per message. This is optional — the app works fully without SMS.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes! Contact support@buyappsonce.com and we'll help you upgrade. For self-hosted, you just pay the difference. For cloud-hosted, we'll prorate your subscription.

Is there a free trial?

We don't offer trials, but we do offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If OpsDeck doesn't work for your needs, email us for a full refund.

What support is included?

Email support is included with all plans. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days. Documentation and setup guides are included with your purchase.
