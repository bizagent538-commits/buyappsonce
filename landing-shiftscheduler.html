Frequently Asked Questions

How much does ShiftScheduler cost per employee?

Nothing! Unlike When I Work ($2.50/user/mo), 7shifts ($3/user/mo), or Deputy ($4.50/user/mo), ShiftScheduler is a flat one-time fee. Add unlimited employees at no extra cost.

Can employees see their schedules on their phones?

Yes! Employees can view schedules through a shared link. Pro version also sends SMS text notifications when schedules are posted.

Can employees request time off?

Yes! The Pro version includes time-off request management. Employees submit requests, you approve or deny with one click.

Does it track labor costs?

Yes! The Pro version shows labor costs as you build schedules. Enter hourly rates and see totals update in real-time.
