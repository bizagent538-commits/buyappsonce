Frequently Asked Questions

Is BookIt really a one-time purchase?

Yes! Pay $49 or $199 once and own it forever. No monthly fees, no annual renewals, no price increases. Unlike Calendly ($12/mo) or Acuity ($20/mo), you never pay again.

Yes! BookIt generates a booking link you can share on your website, social media, or email. Clients see your real-time availability and book themselves.

The Base version sends email confirmations. The Pro version adds SMS text message reminders to reduce no-shows.
