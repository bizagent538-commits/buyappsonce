Frequently Asked Questions

How much does RentRoll cost per unit?

Nothing! Unlike Buildium ($55/mo + per-unit), AppFolio ($1.40/unit/mo), or TenantCloud ($15/mo), RentRoll is a flat one-time fee. Add unlimited properties and units at no extra cost.

Can tenants pay rent through RentRoll?

RentRoll tracks payments you receive through any method (check, Venmo, Zelle, etc). It's a tracking system, not a payment processor—so you avoid those 2-3% fees.

Can tenants submit maintenance requests?

Yes! The Pro version includes a tenant portal where renters can submit and track maintenance requests online.

Does it handle multiple properties?

Yes! The Pro version supports multiple properties in a portfolio view with consolidated reporting across all your rentals.
